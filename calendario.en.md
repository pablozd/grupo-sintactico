## Criteria for organizing sessions

- Each meeting focuses on a single topic.
- The main reading should be accessible to students who are just starting out.
- Whenever possible, complementary readings are added for those who want to go deeper.
- Facilitators don't have to "teach a class": they coordinate the reading and guide the discussion.

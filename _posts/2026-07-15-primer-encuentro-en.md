The first session will present how the group works, its way of operating, and an initial proposal of topics for the reading calendar.

## Discussion points

- What does syntax study?
- What does it mean to analyze a sentence?
- What place does syntax occupy within grammar?
- What minimal tools do we need to get started?

## Tasks for the next session

- Choose the first shared reading text.
- Draft a tentative list of topics.
- Assign facilitators for upcoming sessions.

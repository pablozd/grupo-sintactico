## Possible formats

### Reading sessions

Meetings centered on discussing a previously selected text. Each session includes a short opening presentation, a set of guiding questions, and an open discussion.

### Analysis workshops

Hands-on activities for practicing syntactic analysis tools: segmentation, constituent identification, tree representation, function diagnostics, and comparison of alternative analyses.

### Short talks

Presentations by group members on a problem, a theoretical notion, a language, or a specific article.
